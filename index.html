Pain is beautiful because it is unique to all of us but more so because
it teaches us what we otherwise would not be able to see.
Without it, we would not be able to experience the joy life brings.
What is life without pain?




I chose a theme of angels because they are usually associated with innocence.
I purposefully made drawings on top pupiless as a way to show how we are "blind" to the hardships of life when we are younger,
but when we grow up we fall into bouts of emotional turmoil acompanied with negatives experiences.
I also used parallax scrolling to imitate the falling effect.
Overall, I wanted to share that these tough times help shape us and make us stronger.
Even when life gives us a hard time, we are able to move forward.
